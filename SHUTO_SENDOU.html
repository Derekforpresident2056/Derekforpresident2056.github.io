APPEARANCE

Sendo is a tall, lean, and handsome young man who has tragus-piercing, short, reddish salmon slightly fluffy hair with two side bangs, the one on the left being longer on the right, reaching his chin; meanwhile, the one on the right is reaching his cheek. There are small undercuts underneath and thin pale red eyes with long eyelashes. He is mostly seen with a calm or negative look on his face.


PERSONALITY

Those questioning his position as the ace striker of the U-20 team make Sendo feel insulted, and during the match against Blue Lock, he pushes himself and wants to become better, deciding to aim higher and marry a Hollywood actress instead of a Japanese gravure idol that he previously settled for.

Sendo tends to be arrogant and confident in his own skills. He comes off as annoying, blabbering about all his accomplishments, easily being provoking and loud to anyone. He never hesitates to talk back to people who irritate him, making him seem like an assertive person. He refuses to let go of his ego, having a big passion for soccer.
